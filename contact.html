About the Stadium

The National Stadium, located in the heart of the city, is a world-class sports venue equipped with modern facilities, a capacity of over 80,000 spectators, and advanced technologies for a memorable match experience. It has hosted numerous international events and is set to be the centerpiece for the World Cup 2025 finals.

Fans can enjoy seamless access through public transport, diverse food courts, and a vibrant fan zone just outside the main gates. Whether you're cheering for your favorite team or simply soaking in the atmosphere, the National Stadium promises an unforgettable experience.
